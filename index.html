WELCOME

Veda's story began in 2005 with a clear and unique vision of the future of fast food. Recognizing the importance of healthy living, a desire for flavor, and authenticity, Veda's menu was born to make no compromise accidentally.

Since our humble beginnings as a family-run eatery in Toronto, Veda has been able to maintain its unique healthy Indian fast food concept in a quick-serve platform, without compromising on quality and taste. We are grateful for the loyalty of our guests who have supported us in being able to deliver healthy, fast traditional fast food Indian cuisine. We hope you enjoy your Veda Experience!
